01 Introduction

Slate ("we", "our", or "the app") is a local-first, offline, encrypted note-taking application developed by iefanx. This Privacy Policy describes our data practices in relation to the Slate mobile application and its companion web application hosted at https://iefanx.github.io/slate/.

Core Principle

Slate is architecturally designed to be serverless and offline-first. We have no servers, no databases, and no cloud infrastructure that could receive or store your personal data. This is not just a policy — it is a technical guarantee built into the product.

02 Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever. Specifically, Slate does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • The content of your notes or documents
  • Your location or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics, session data, or behavioral metrics
  • Crash reports or error logs sent to any server
  • Advertising identifiers or targeting data

03 How Your Data Is Stored

All data created within Slate is stored exclusively on your local device using the browser's IndexedDB storage API. Your notes are encrypted using AES-GCM-256 encryption with keys derived from your master password via PBKDF2, a cryptographic standard used by security professionals worldwide.

  • Your master password never leaves your device
  • Your encrypted notes are stored only in your browser's local storage
  • Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app permanently deletes all notes
  • No backup copies exist anywhere other than on your device

04 Sharing Feature (Zero-DB Links)

Slate includes an optional "Share" feature that encodes note content into a compressed URL fragment using LZ-string compression. When you use this feature:

  • Note content is compressed and embedded directly in the URL hash fragment (after the # symbol)
  • URL hash fragments are never sent to any server — they exist only in the browser
  • Sharing a link means only the person who receives the link can read the content
  • We do not log, track, or have access to any shared links

Important Note

Sharing a Slate link sends the note content in compressed form to whoever receives the link. Only share links with people you trust, as anyone with the link can read the note content.

05 Third-Party Services

Slate does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking services. The app is served via GitHub Pages, a static hosting platform operated by GitHub, Inc. By accessing the web version of Slate, you may be subject to GitHub's Privacy Policy regarding standard web server access logs.

We have no control over and take no responsibility for GitHub's data practices. GitHub's access logs may record standard HTTP data such as IP addresses, browser user agents, and request timestamps.

06 Children's Privacy

Slate does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because we collect zero personal data from any users, the app is safe for all age groups from an information collection standpoint.

However, parents and guardians should supervise their children's use of any application. If you believe a child has provided personal information through any means associated with Slate, please contact us immediately.

07 Security

The security of your notes is paramount to Slate's design philosophy. We implement the following measures:

  • AES-GCM-256 symmetric encryption for all stored notes
  • PBKDF2 key derivation with high iteration count to protect against brute-force attacks
  • No network transmission of your encrypted or unencrypted note content
  • No third-party code that could exfiltrate your data

While we implement strong technical safeguards, please remember that the security of your notes ultimately depends on the strength of your master password and the security of your physical device.

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since Slate collects no user data, we have no mechanism to notify individual users of changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

Continued use of Slate after any modifications to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acknowledgment of the modified policy.
